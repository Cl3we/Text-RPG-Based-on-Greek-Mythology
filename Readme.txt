ABOUT THIS PROJECT
------------------------
This project implements an interactive board game fashioned after the story of the Odyssey. The users control 1 player each as they 
attempt to survive and progress through board in order to reach ithica and win. They must defeat monsters on various islands
and survive the harsh conditions of the open ocean.
